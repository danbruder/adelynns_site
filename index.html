#6 Do you know about the Transcontinental Railroad?

Here are some facts:

  • The completion of the Transcontinental Railroad was on May 10th, 1869.
  • The completion was placed in Utah when workers from the west and the east worked together and met in the middle.
  • Native Americans were forced to move their land because the Transcontinental Railroad was going through where they lived.
  • African Americans were forced to work on the Transcontinental Railroad.
  • Before the Transcontinental Railroad was built, people has wagons to take them across to the west that took usually more than six months! After it was built, it took just seven weeks! That's a lot of change!
